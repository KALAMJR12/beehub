About Us

Our Mission

The Bello El-Rufai Empowerment Hub (BEE-Hub) is dedicated to empowering young people through comprehensive development programs that focus on education, skills acquisition, and character building. We believe that investing in youth is investing in the future of our community.

Our Vision

To create a generation of self-reliant, educated, and socially responsible young leaders who will contribute positively to society and drive sustainable development in their communities.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we have reached over 1,000 young people through various programs including vocational training, leadership development, educational scholarships, and community service initiatives. Our programs have helped participants gain valuable skills, access education, and build brighter futures.

Core Programs & Initiatives

Primary constituency initiatives driving real-world impact across Kaduna North

Educational Sponsorships

Comprehensive support for Kaduna North students including WAEC/NECO examination fee coverage and tertiary institution tuition scholarships. We remove financial barriers so talented youth can focus on their education and unlock their potential.

Facilitators: Bello El-Rufai Foundation, Kaduna State Government
Execution Date: Ongoing Initiative (Since 2023)

Financial Inclusion & POS Deployments

Partnership with MTN to train and equip youth as independent retail and POS earning merchants. This initiative creates sustainable income opportunities by providing skills training, merchant equipment, and ongoing support for young entrepreneurs.

Facilitators: MTN Nigeria, Bello El-Rufai Foundation
Execution Date: Active Partnership (Launched Q2 2024)

Green Energy & Skill Acquisition

Solar installation training tracks in partnership with Interface Africa, combined with agricultural pump distribution initiatives. Youth gain valuable green technology skills while supporting sustainable development across rural and urban communities.

Facilitators: Interface Africa, Renewable Energy Association
Execution Date: Strategic Alliance (Piloted Q3 2024)
